About Us


Welcome

to Kedai Makan Vegetarian Thien En



Founded by sisters Sumi Yeni and Lim Lie Gek, Thien En Vegetarian Restaurant opened its doors on October 22, 2018. Born out of a shared passion for introducing a variety of vegetarian and vegan cuisines to the world, Thien En offers a delightful mix of Chinese, Korean, Western, and Japanese dishes. Sumi and Lim have always believed in the power of good food to bring people together. "We wanted to introduce a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes to a wider audience," says Sumi. With this vision in mind, they have honed their culinary skills to create a diverse and exciting menu that appeals to all palates.


At Thien En, we pride ourselves on using fresh, high-quality ingredients to craft meals that are not only healthy but also incredibly delicious. Each dish is thoughtfully prepared to provide a memorable dining experience, whether you're a long-time vegetarian, a curious foodie, or someone looking to explore plant-based options.

Join us at Thien En Vegetarian Restaurant and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the rich flavors and diverse traditions of vegetarian cuisine. We look forward to welcoming you!
